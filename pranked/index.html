Quick safety checklist
  • Check the URL: fake pages often use look-alike domains. When in doubt, type the site manually.
  • HTTPS ≠ safe: a lock icon only means encrypted — not trustworthy by itself.
  • Never enter credentials from a link in email/text. Go directly to the service’s official site/app.
  • Look for urgency or threats: “verify now or lose access” is a classic red flag.
  • Enable MFA/2FA: even if you slip up, it can block the login.
